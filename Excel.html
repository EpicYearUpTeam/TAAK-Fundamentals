Microsoft Excel

Overview

This lesson will give viewers the fundamental basics necessary to navigate through excel and stand out in the workplace. Included in this video are quick tutorials on the Excel functions that companies value in the workplace. There will also be still images highlighting the important features. The end result of this video is for viewers to gain knowledge of an important Microsoft program that adds value to the individual as an employee.
