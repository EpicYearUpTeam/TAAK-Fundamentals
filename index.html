How we came to be

What started out as an assignment to create a 10-page website resulted into the art laid about on the monitor in front of you. When creativity and ambition come together through a subtle application of elbow grease, all that you are left with are the tools necessary to prosper in life.

Meaning of TAAK

The acronym, TAAK means “Teaching all awesome knowledge”, the ultimate purpose of this site (fortunately, our very names expedited the process). We want to empower you through the use of technology, and give you the necessary tools to be successful in your personal endeavors. Think of every puzzle piece that you see in the background as a fundamental characteristic of what we're trying to accomplish here. It may be because our ambition is lofty, but there are endless pieces. And yet it does'nt end there. We will continue to thrive as long as there is space for knowledge to reign supreme above ignorance. And you can be a part of this growth; after all, you're the missing piece.

Mission Statement

Empowered by education and on the atTAAK against the forces of lethargy, we encourage all to share in our delight of sharing knowledge and becoming more powerful in the process.
