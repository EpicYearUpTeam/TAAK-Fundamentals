Ke'Aira Hankerson

Summary

My name is Ke’Aira Hankerson, and I look at Year Up as a pure opportunity for those who are open for change and are honest with themselves about what they must do to reach a new level of individual success. I want to leave a monumental footprint on earth that people will never forget. I want to build my own legacy to pass down to future generations; for my daughter Katalina, and also for Year Up as thanks for giving me a hand-up and not a hand-out. I have struggled for many years, but with this program I have been supplied with resources to move forward in one of the best “In demand” fields in the world- information technology! I am excited that I’m growing constantly. Consider that I was a high school dropout. I was just another homeless youth. I am a single mom. However, all these stereotypical labels and depressing statistics don’t mean anything because I am now the true definition of E.P.I.C; Empowered, Professional, In Demand, and Career Ready! (Fitting picture, huh?)
